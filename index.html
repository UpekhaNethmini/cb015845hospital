Services

Emergency and Trauma Care

  • Immediate medical attention for critical conditions like heart attacks, strokes, accidents, and injuries.
  • Open 24/7 to handle urgent and life-threatening situations.
  • Equipped with specialized staff and facilities such as intensive care units (ICUs) and emergency rooms (ERs).

Outpatient Services

  • Medical consultations, diagnostic tests, and minor procedures that do not require an overnight stay.
  • Includes specialties like dermatology, ophthalmology, cardiology, and orthopedics.
  • Designed for routine health checkups, follow-up visits, and preventive care.

Surgical Services

  • Performs both minor and major surgeries in dedicated operating rooms.
  • Can include general surgery, orthopedic surgery, neurosurgery, and cardiovascular surgery.
  • Provides pre-operative and post-operative care to ensure patient recovery.

Maternity and Pediatric Care

  • Comprehensive care for women during pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum.
  • Neonatal care for newborns and pediatric services for children's health.
  • Includes labor rooms, birthing centers, and facilities for handling high-risk pregnancies and deliveries.
