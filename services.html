Our Services

Our hospital offers a diverse range of services designed to provide the highest quality care for our patients. We are equipped with a 24/7 emergency department to handle life-threatening conditions, ensuring immediate attention in critical moments. Our advanced surgical services cover a wide array of procedures, while our maternity and neonatal care provide specialized support for mothers and newborns. We also offer comprehensive diagnostic imaging and radiology to assist in accurate diagnoses, alongside rehabilitation services to help patients recover strength and mobility after illness or surgery. Mental health services are available for both inpatient and outpatient care, supporting individuals with conditions such as depression, anxiety, and more. Additionally, our pharmacy ensures safe and effective medication management, offering guidance and support for chronic conditions. With a dedicated team of healthcare professionals, we are committed to delivering compassionate, patient-centered care across all services.

Emergency Care Services

The emergency department (ER) provides 24/7 care for urgent, life-threatening conditions like heart attacks, severe injuries, strokes, and respiratory distress.

Surgical Services

Hospitals offer a range of surgical procedures, from routine operations to complex surgeries. These include both inpatient and outpatient (day surgery) services.

Maternity and Neonatal Care

Maternity services ensure safe and supportive care for expectant mothers and their newborns. Hospitals provide prenatal, childbirth, and postnatal care.

Diagnostic Imaging and Radiology

Radiology departments provide critical diagnostic tools that help doctors visualize internal structures of the body for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

Rehabilitation Services

Rehabilitation services help patients recover from illness, injury, or surgery by improving mobility, strength, and function. These services can be provided on an inpatient or outpatient basis.

Mental Health and Psychiatric Services

Hospitals offer mental health services, including inpatient care for severe cases and outpatient therapy for conditions like depression, anxiety, and schizophrenia.
